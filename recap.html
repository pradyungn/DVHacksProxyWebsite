What was DVHacks 1 like?

Last year we held our first hackathon in January 2019 and it was an overwhelming success, given our relative novelty. We had over 150 hackers and raised over $10k. We had numerous workshops covering different useful APIs as well as machine learning and AI applications. We plan to build off the success of last year, and make DVHacks bigger, better, and just as fun as last time!
